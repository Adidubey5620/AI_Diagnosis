# AI Medical Diagnosis Assistant - Pitch Deck

## 1. Problem Statement
- **Global Healthcare Shortage**: The WHO projects a shortfall of 10 million health workers by 2030.
- **Diagnostic Errors**: Diagnostic errors affect 1 in 20 US adults annually.
- **Access Disparity**: Rural and underserved areas lack access to specialized radiology services.

## 2. Our Solution
**AI Diagnosis Assistant**: An intelligent, web-based platform that democratizes access to medical imaging analysis.
- **Instant Analysis**: Uses Google's Gemini 2.0 Vision to provide preliminary analysis in seconds.
- **Accessibility**: Works on any device, with multi-language support and accessibility features.
- **Empowerment**: Assists general practitioners and informs patients (with appropriate disclaimers).

## 3. Technology Stack
- **Frontend**: React, TypeScript, Vite, Tailwind CSS (Modern, fast, responsive).
- **Backend**: FastAPI, Python (High-performance, easy AI integration).
- **AI Core**: Google Gemini 2.0 Pro Vision (State-of-the-art multimodal reasoning).
- **Infrastructure**: Cloud-native, scalable architecture.

## 4. Key Features
- **Multi-Modality**: Supports X-rays, MRIs, and CT scans.
- **Interactive Viewer**: Advanced tools for detailed inspection.
- **Automated Reporting**: Professional PDF generation for clinical workflows.
- **Emergency Protocol**: Real-time detection of critical conditions.

## 5. Impact & Market
- **Target Users**: Clinics, Telemedicine Providers, Medical Students, Rural Hospitals.
- **Metrics**: 
    - 95% reduction in preliminary triage time.
    - Potential to serve millions in underserved regions.

## 6. Roadmap
- **Q3 2026**: DICOM PACS integration.
- **Q4 2026**: Mobile App (iOS/Android).
- **Q1 2027**: FDA Clearance application & Clinical Trials.
